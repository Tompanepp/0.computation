human language and word meaning (Manning, 2019): 
    human language serves to connect humans in the "human network".
        human language = networking language
            slow network relative to modern computer networks.
    writing is ~5000 years old
    language is ~100000 years old
    our chief advantage as an animal is our ability to communicate and write knowledge for spatial and temporal transmission.
        - spatial: to different people, countries, etc
        - temporal: for posterity
